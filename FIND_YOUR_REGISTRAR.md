# How to Find and Log In to Your Domain Registrar

## Quick Ways to Find Your Registrar

### Method 1: Check Your Email
1. Search your email for:
   - "roovert.com"
   - "domain registration"
   - "domain purchase"
   - "domain receipt"
2. Look for emails from registrars like:
   - Namecheap
   - GoDaddy
   - Google Domains
   - Cloudflare
   - Name.com
   - Domain.com
   - Hover
   - etc.

### Method 2: Check Your Payment Records
1. Check your credit card or PayPal statements
2. Look for charges related to domain registration
3. The company name will tell you the registrar

### Method 3: Use WHOIS Lookup
1. Go to: https://whois.net or https://whois.com
2. Enter: `roovert.com`
3. Look for "Registrar:" field - this shows who manages your domain

### Method 4: Try Common Registrars
Try logging in to these popular registrars:

**Namecheap**
- Website: https://www.namecheap.com
- Login: https://www.namecheap.com/myaccount/login/
- Look for: "Sign In" button

**GoDaddy**
- Website: https://www.godaddy.com
- Login: https://sso.godaddy.com
- Look for: "Sign In" button

**Google Domains**
- Website: https://domains.google
- Login: https://domains.google.com/registrar
- Uses your Google account

**Cloudflare**
- Website: https://www.cloudflare.com
- Login: https://dash.cloudflare.com/login
- Look for: "Log In" button

**Name.com**
- Website: https://www.name.com
- Login: https://www.name.com/login
- Look for: "Sign In" button

## How to Log In

### If You Remember Your Account:
1. Go to the registrar's website
2. Click "Sign In" or "Log In"
3. Enter your email/username and password
4. If you forgot password, click "Forgot Password"

### If You Don't Remember Your Account:
1. **Try password reset:**
   - Go to registrar's login page
   - Click "Forgot Password"
   - Enter the email you used to buy the domain
   - Check your email for reset link

2. **Check your email:**
   - Search for emails from the registrar
   - Look for account creation or purchase confirmation emails
   - These usually contain your account email

3. **Contact support:**
   - Most registrars have support chat/email
   - Provide proof of domain ownership
   - They can help you recover access

## What to Do After Logging In

Once you're logged in:
1. Find your domain list (usually "My Domains" or "Domain List")
2. Click on `roovert.com`
3. Look for "DNS Settings", "DNS Management", or "Advanced DNS"
4. Add the DNS records Vercel provided:
   - A record: `@` → `216.198.79.1`
   - CNAME record: `www` → `cname.vercel-dns.com`

## Still Can't Find It?

If you can't find your registrar:
1. **Use WHOIS lookup** (most reliable):
   - Visit: https://whois.net
   - Enter: `roovert.com`
   - Check the "Registrar" field

2. **Check domain expiration emails:**
   - Registrars send renewal reminders
   - Search your email for "domain renewal" or "expiring soon"

3. **Check your browser's saved passwords:**
   - Your browser might have saved login info
   - Check password manager if you use one
